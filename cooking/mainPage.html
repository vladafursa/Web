Why learn cooking?

First of all, let's determine what cooking means. Cooking is a process of preparing food for the next eating of it.
Everybody should know how to cook. Eventhough many restaurants exist, there can be a situation, when you will need to cook something. By cooking food yourself, you can choose ingredients yourself, make the food more healthy and cut out calories. Moreover, it saves money. Cooking isn't hard,on the contrary, it can be fun.
